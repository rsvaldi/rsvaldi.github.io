Venue

The lectures are held at the Centre for Mathematical Sciences, Wilberforce Road, Cambridge, CB3 0WA, check the schedule for the precise room. Here you can find some information on how to get to the CMS.

Transport

Cambridge can be reached by plane (the closest airport is Stansted, just a 30-minute train ride away), train (if you are coming through London then you may want to check trains from Kings Cross-St Pancras or Liverpool St) or bus.
Train tickets can be bought through National Rail. For bus tickets, please consult National Express.

Cambridge is a rather small town. It is quite easy to walk around town and get a chance to see all the beautiful historical spots that you may encounter on the way. If you wish to take a bus, please consult this website.
If you wish to get a taxi to CMS, the average fare shall be around 9GBP. Taxis can be found right outside the main entrance of the train stration and at different locations in the city centre.
