Other opportunities:
  • INDAM postdoctoral positions (deadline for application: February 9th 2023);
  • Bando Rita Levi Montalcini (currently closed): this program awards a tenure track position to researchers that have been working outside of Italy for at least the 3 year previous to the closing date of the call, at an Italian university of choice of the applicant;

If you are interested in applying to one of the above opportunities with the University of Milan as your hosting institution, possibly choosing me as your mentor, please, do contact me in due time.
