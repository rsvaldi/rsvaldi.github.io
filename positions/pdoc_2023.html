One 2-year postdoctoral position in algebraic geometry (deadline for application: January 3oth 2023, extended to January 31st due to technical problems on the file submission webpage).

One 2-year postdoctoral position is available in the Algebraic Geometry group at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Milan.

Research topics

The position is funded by the university on the themes of the project “Boundedness and moduli problems in algebraic geometry and foliation theory” that is coordinated by Luca Tasin and myself.
Thus, we are looking for people whose research interests are related to birational geometry, geometry of varieties with trivial canonical bundle, algebraic foliations, boundedness problems (all intended in the widest possible sense).

Salary

The gross salary for the position is €21000/year which, after taxes, corresponds to about €1550/month.
A mobility allowance of €600/month will be added to the salary for those that satisfy the following 2 conditions: they have obtained their doctoral degree, or are about to obtain one, from a university outside of Italy, and, if already graduated with a PhD, are currently residing outside of Italy. Thus, the corresponding gross salary will then increase to €28200/year, which after taxes corresponds to €2050/month.

Deadline for application

Deadline for applications is January 30th, at 12h00 (pm) Italian time (aka CET). The deadline has been extended to the same hour of January 31st, due to technical issues on the application submission webpage. In case of further problems, please contact assegni.postdoc@unimi.it.

Starting date and duration

There is no fixed starting date for the position and there is some flexibility in choosing one. Once the results of the evaluations will be published, the person who is awarded the position will be contacted by the University of Milan with a contract offer. At that time, they can opt to postpone the starting date proposed by the university by up to 90 days, in agreement with the PIs of the project.

Research in AG at University of Milan

The Department of Mathematics at the University of Milan currently has a large research group in Algebraic Geometry currently featuring 16 permanent members and 5 postdoctoral associates/researchers.
Research themes represented in the group cover a wide range of topics in modern AG, e.g., birational geometry, hyperkaehler varieties, stability conditions and moduli spaces, Hodge theory, algebraic foliations, derived categories, and many more. We organise a weekly seminar and reading groups during the semester.
More information on the group and its members are available here.

Application procedure

To access the application system, click here. Set up an account and then access the system. We have written a document to explain how to complete the application procedure which is available here.
You shall need to upload the following documents:
  • a research statement;
  • PhD thesis in the form of a pdf file, if already defended, carrying a signature of the head of department, or head of the graduate program, or an abstract of the thesis, if not yet defended, signed by the the head of department, or head of the graduate program;
  • up to 6 publications of the candidate's choice in the form of a pdf file;
  • a pdf file listing all 6 publications together with a brief explanation for the choice of the publications;
  • curriculum vitae in in the form of a pdf file (according to the template provided here, you can take some freedom and use a different template as long as the relevant info is there);
  • scan of a valid ID.
No letter of recommendation is required as part of the application procedure.
Please, do notice that, if not already held at the time of application, you will need to have obtain your doctoral degree by the start of the contract.
